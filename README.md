# Designed to Think. Built to Win.

> A competition robotics project focused on intelligence, execution, and teamwork.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

**Designed to Think. Built to Win.** isn’t just a slogan — it’s the philosophy behind our robot.

Our goal was to engineer a robot that could make smart decisions in real time, execute them reliably under pressure, and adapt to the game through strategy-driven design. This project represents the integration of purposeful code, optimized hardware, and strong team collaboration.

---

## 💡 Inspiration

We wanted our robot to do more than react. By emphasizing decision-making, adaptability, and consistency, we aimed to build a robot that *thinks first* and *wins through execution*.

---

## 🤖 What It Does

- Executes intelligent autonomous routines
- Responds quickly and consistently during driver control
- Optimizes scoring efficiency through strategy-driven design
- Maintains reliability under match pressure

## 🧠 How We Built It

1. **Game Analysis & Strategy**
   - Identified key scoring paths and decision points
2. **Software Architecture**
   - Designed adaptive logic and autonomous behavior
3. **Mechanical Design**
   - Balanced speed, power, and durability
4. **Iteration & Testing**
   - Tested under real match conditions
   - Refined based on performance data
5. **Team Integration**
   - Continuous communication and collaboration
